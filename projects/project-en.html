Description

Mission
Development of rural settlements of Artsakh and Armenia.

Goal
Establishment of minicomplex farms in rural areas of Artsakh and Armenia.

Tasks
1. Establishment of a farm consisting 50 chickens, 50 turkeys and 50 rabbits.
2. Building a farm for 200 sq. meters within 2-3 months.
3. Purchase of animals.
4. Educate residents in the peculiarities of caring for animals.
5. Organization of the process for reaching self-sufficiency 3 months after the launch.

Advantages
This set of animals is optimal not only in terms of efficiency, but also in terms of organization of the economy.
Meat and eggs are very much in demand in the local market, and can also become part of the farm organizer's diet.
Such a farm requires the work of 3-4 people and can satisfy a family of up to 7 people.
With the right organization, after 3 months the farm can reach self-sufficiency and can generate income from sales of its products up to $ 600 per month.

For residents of rural areas of Artsakh and Armenia, this amount of money is significant.

During the year, the founder of the farm must return 50 chickens, 50 turkeys and 50 rabbits to the “Armgyugh” project, which will be used to launch a similar project in other territories. In addition, within 5 years, in every New Year, the founder undertakes to send the investor “Parcel from Grandma” gifts made from natural products
