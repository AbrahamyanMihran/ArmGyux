History

"Armgyugh" is a social project, that helps make venture capital investments in the micro-businesses of the communities of Artsakh and Armenia. Micro-business venture is when investors invest, help the founders to create a micro-business, and the founders undertake to send a certain amount of products from the project every year or to participate in the creation of a similar micro-business on pre-agreed deadlines with investors. "Armgyugh" was founded immediately after the 44-day Artsakh war, in 2022 by the efforts of Hayk Trunyan and other like-minded people from Rostov-on-Don city. The project aims to help families consisting of many children displaced from the occupied territories of Artsakh, who lost their fathers during the war.
Our goal is to create comfortable conditions for residents in their own settlements so that they do not leave their communities, launch micro-businesses, be provided with permanent jobs and be involved in the network of all Armenians. This program contributes to the emergence, generation and realization of business ideas in communities of Artsakh and Armenia.
